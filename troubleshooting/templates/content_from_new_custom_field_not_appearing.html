Content from my new Custom Field is not appearing on the page.

A new custom field was created and entries were published with content in that custom field; however, content from the custom field does not get output to the page.

Displaying Content from Custom Fields

Ensure that the custom field is being called within the template. For example, if the custom field is named "extended" then it needs to be called in the template like so: {exp:channel:entries}
{extended}
{/exp:channel:entries}
