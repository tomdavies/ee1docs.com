Email Module Tags

ExpressionEngine includes an Email Module in order to provide the ability to create contact forms and tell-a-friend forms.

  • The Contact Form tag allows your visitors to send you email directly from your site.
  • The Tell-a-Friend tag allows your visitors to share your content via email with someone else.

NOTE: The Email Module is not installed by default, so before using these tags make sure it is installed via the Modules Page.
