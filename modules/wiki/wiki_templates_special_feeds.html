Wiki Template, Special RSS and ATOM Feeds Section

These sections generate the RSS and ATOM feeds for the wiki. You can use any of the variables available on the Special Recent Changes section to format the feeds.

The best approach is to review the formatting of the feeds in the default template file and then modify them for your own use.
