Photo Gallery Control Panel

The Photo Gallery Module (Control Panel: Modules > Photo Gallery) allows you to create, edit, and display image galleries. Images may be grouped together in different galleries, categorized within those galleries, and even manipulated.

Once you are within a specific gallery, you may access these sections:

  • New Entry
  • Batch Entries
  • View Entries
  • Categories
  • Toolbox
  • Preferences
