Updated Sites Module

This Updated Sites Module allows other sites to ping your site when they have created new content. You can then display a "recently updated sites" list based on the sites that send you pings.

The module will accept either a standard Weblogs.com style ping (which is most common) or an Extended Ping.

There are two areas of information for this module:

  • Receiving Pings
  • Displaying Pings
